What it is
A writing platform with assignment posting, typing-pattern analysis, and per-submission replay, all in one place.
Who it's for
Elementary schools and their teachers — the age where critical-thinking habits are still being formed, and where AI use is most distorting.
Why now
A generation is growing up assuming every problem gets outsourced to a chatbot. Good tools can protect the developmental work that classrooms are meant to do.
